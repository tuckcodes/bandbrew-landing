How it works

In three steps

Join a Circuit

~ 30 minutes

Circuits are groups of similar venues (such as "Wineries of Colorado") who have joined together to share entertainment talent via the circuit - it works similar to joining a Meetup.com group. If a band drops the ball with one venue it will impact their eligibility with another venue on the same circuit.
Continue

Add the Team

~ 15 minutes

Venues can add team members to assist with managing gigs and brew selections. The account can be modified to fit your venue's entertainment needs. The first account is at no charge, along with numerous free-to-use services. We value startup breweries and venues - so here's to an "always free" startup policy.
Just one more

Sponsor a Band

~ 15 minutes

Bands can only enter a circuit via sponsorship. They are sponsored by venues as a voucher - think of sports labels such as surfing, skateboarding, etc. This ensures that the msucians shared across circuits are nothing other than top quality entertainment. The band's use their mobile app as an accountability system - similar to an Uber driver.
